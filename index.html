The Group Attachment Based Intervention

The Group Attachment Intervention study was developed jointly by The New School for Social Research, Center for Attachment, and the Albert Einstein College of Medicine’s Center for Babies, Toddlers and Families in 2006. It’s goal is to break the cycle of child maltreatment in vulnerable and low income families by intervening before the child turns 3 years old.
