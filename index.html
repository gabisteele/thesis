Static Data Sharing

Social Science research findings are always shared using static platforms. Writing a paper and uploading it as a PDF to scholarly.org is still static!

Human Centered Design

Having a stronger focus on human centered design when portraying human centered data is an essential next step for the Social Science community. Much of the work being conducted is shared and taught without implementation of HCI design principles and consideration of the way in which humans are able to succesfully absorb large amounts of information.

Raising the Status Quo

Those research groups that do begin employing data visualization techniques when sharing their findings will set the bar higher throughout the entire psychological community.
